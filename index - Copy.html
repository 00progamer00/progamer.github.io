What makes Lighting Host different from regular Minecraft hosting?

We specialize in optimizing Minecraft servers specifically for lighting calculations. Our servers are fine-tuned to handle complex lighting updates, shaders, and dynamic lighting plugins with minimal performance impact, something general-purpose hosts don't focus on.

Which shaders do you support?

We support all popular shaders including SEUS (all versions), BSL, Complementary, Sildur's, and more. Our control panel includes one-click installation for most shader packs with optimized configurations.

Can I use lighting plugins like Dynamic Lights?

Absolutely! We've optimized our servers to work seamlessly with lighting plugins. Popular options like Dynamic Lights, LightAPI, and GlowAPI work perfectly with our hosting.

How do you prevent lighting lag in large builds?

Our servers use specialized configurations that optimize how Minecraft handles lighting updates. We implement efficient light recalculation algorithms and provide tools to easily fix lighting errors that might cause lag.
